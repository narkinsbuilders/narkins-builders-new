## Hidden Costs You Must Factor In

### One-Time Expenses

Registration Fees: 1-2% of property value
Legal Documentation: PKR 50,000 - 100,000

Maintenance Charges: PKR 8,000 - 15,000
Utilities: PKR 15,000 - 25,000 (electric, gas, water)

Budget Reality: Add 15-20% to purchase price for total ownership cost in first year.
